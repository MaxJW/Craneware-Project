About

This application provides a quick and easy way to compare prices of various conditions and treatments between many hospitals across the US. Simply enter you DRG code or description and apply any of the filters you require to see the results for supporting hospitals and their relevent prices and distances from your location, or specified Zip code.

DRG Code

DRG stands for Diagnosis Related Groups. DRGs are used to classify hospital cases in to different groups. These are used to identify "products" that a hospital provides e.g a kidney transplant. These groupings are used for establishing how much Medicare pays the hospital for each "product".
Unsure of your DRG code? Click Here To find out what DRG your treatment or condition is priced under.

MCC/CC

MCC - Stands for Major Complication or Comorbidity. Comorbidity is refered to when the DRG being referenced is used as a secondary diagnosis
CC - Stands for Complication or Comborbidity. Comorbidity is refered to when the DRG being referenced is used as a secondary diagnosis
